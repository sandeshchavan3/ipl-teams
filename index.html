IPL


The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India, organized by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI). It's the most popular and richest cricket league in the world, featuring ten franchise teams. The 2025 season runs from March 22 to May 25, with 74 matches played across 13 venues. The current champion is Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR), having won their third title in the previous season.
